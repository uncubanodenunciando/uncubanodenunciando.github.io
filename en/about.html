About Un Cubano Denunciando

Welcome to Un Cubano Denunciando, a platform committed to the search for truth and the defense of freedom in Cuba. The creator of this space has decided to remain anonymous for security reasons.
Our Mission:
At Un Cubano Denunciando, we are dedicated to contributing to the fight for Cuban freedom and defending press freedom. We believe in the importance of exposing acts of extreme violence, especially murders, to shed light on reality and encourage informed dialogue.
Diversity of Perspectives:
We recognize the existence of different perspectives and approaches in the coverage of events in Cuba. Un Cubano Denunciando strives to present a variety of opinions, highlighting both reports from the dictatorship's media and those from independent media. We believe that this diversity allows our users to form their own opinions in an informed manner.
Creator Anonymity:
The creator of Un Cubano Denunciando has chosen to remain anonymous to ensure his personal safety. This decision does not affect the integrity of the information provided, which is based on verifiable sources and various media outlets.
Detailed Statistics:
At Un Cubano Denunciando, we strive to provide detailed statistics on murders in Cuba. It is important to note that the statistics presented on this website are calculated automatically by our algorithm. We are committed to maintaining transparency and integrity in the data collection and reporting process to provide a complete view of the situation. We explored data by province, sex, method used, age and month of the event.
Your Participation:
We invite our users to participate in the conversation. Join us on our social networks to share your opinions and comments in a respectful manner. Additionally, we encourage you to corroborate or provide additional information about the cases we present. We believe in the importance of active participation to build an informed and engaged community. Your contribution is valuable in our mission to seek the truth and promote freedom in Cuba.
